Top 3 Types of Oranges

  1. Navel
    • Known for their ease of peeling and are a popular choice for eating out of hand.
  2. Valencia
    • Popular choice for juicing and are unique in that they are in season during the summer.
  3. Cara Cara
    • Offers an exceptionally sweet flavor and a delightful tropical aroma, resembling a cross between a navel and grapefruit, with a pinkish-red flesh due to the presence of lycopene.
